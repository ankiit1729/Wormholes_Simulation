Why is α_def negative?

From Eq. (36) of the paper:
α_def = −π + 2∫ dφ/dr · dr

For these wormhole solutions the integral 2∫ < π, so α_def < 0.

This means photons are deflected away from the throat — the wormhole acts as a diverging lens, not a focusing one.

Unlike a black hole (α_def → +∞), here exit rays spread outward, wider than they entered. The photon trajectories in the 3D view reflect this correctly.
